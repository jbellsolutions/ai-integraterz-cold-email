# Capstone Positioning

## What it is (1-line)

Free expert AI implementation on a real workflow problem, run by an AI Integraterz AICO candidate under Justin's Microsoft-certified oversight, in exchange for a real project the candidate can certify on.

## Why this is credible (the load-bearing fact)

This isn't a "free trial" or a "loss-leader." It exists because every AICO candidate must complete a real implementation project on a real business to graduate. The certification page is public: https://aiintegraterz.com/certificationjourney.

That's why we can offer free expert work without it being suspicious — the project is the cost of running the certification process well. We earn nothing on the project itself.

## Identity shift to land in the host's mind

From: *"another sales pitch wearing a free-help costume"*
To: *"a real project I get for free, run under real oversight, with no follow-up sales pressure unless I ask"*

## Frame

The host is doing the candidate a favor. The candidate is doing the host a favor. Both true.

We are explicit about that. The email should not over-sell what the host gets and should not under-describe what they commit to. Honesty is the whole frame.

## What makes the offer different from a free consulting trial

- Microsoft-certified oversight from Justin throughout — every Capstone has Justin in the background, not just the candidate alone.
- Pre-built role kits the candidate deploys on day one — they're not figuring out where to start from a blank slate.
- The host keeps the working solution at the end.
- No-obligation after-conversation: if they want more help, we can talk; if not, no follow-up sales pressure.

## Awareness levels

- **Unaware** (no AI strategy): lead with a specific workflow_pain we know they have.
- **Problem-aware** (knows AI could help, hasn't acted): lead with the role kit / oversight angle.
- **Solution-aware** (tried AI tools that "almost work"): lead with "you don't need another tool, you need someone to run it."
- **Most-aware** (already considering hiring): lead with the active_job_posting + Capstone fit.

## What Copy squad must preserve

1. **Reference the certification process URL** (https://aiintegraterz.com/certificationjourney) somewhere in the sequence. Hard rule.
2. **Honest about the timeline** — 6-10 weeks for a Capstone, longer than a paid 30-day sprint. Don't bury this.
3. **No "free trial" language.** This is not a trial of a paid thing.
4. **No promised follow-up sales.** "We will not, unless they ask."
5. **Closing-context line** if there's room: *"If you like the work and want more help, we can help you with that. That's not the goal of the Capstone. The goal is the project, the candidate's certification, and you getting your problem solved."*
