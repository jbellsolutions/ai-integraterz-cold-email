# Capstone Campaign

The Capstone is **NOT a paid offer.** It is part of AI Integraterz's certification process — every AICO candidate must complete a real implementation project on a real business to graduate. We use cold email to find host businesses for those projects.

The host business gets free expert AI implementation work backed by Justin's Microsoft-certified oversight. The candidate gets a real project to certify on. Some hosts become paying clients afterward; most do not, and that's fine. The point of the Capstone email is the project, not the upsell.

## When Strategy squad picks this angle

Auto-pick when:
- ICP = SMB 1-30 employees with revenue
- Has at least one well-defined operational problem an AICO could meaningfully address in 4-8 weeks
- Owner/operator can do a 1-hour intake + weekly 30-min check-ins
- Active job posting AICO could substitute for, OR public post about a workflow problem, OR recent expansion creating ops drag

Disqualify (universal):
- Pre-revenue / no team / no real workflow
- Already deeply embedded with another agency on the same problem
- Problem too vague to scope in 1 hour
- Industry on the AI Integraterz "do not pitch" list (gambling, adult, restricted cannabis, weapons)

## How Copy squad uses this folder

The Capstone email rules are stricter than the paid offers — read [write-rules.md](write-rules.md) before generating. Hard requirement: the email must reference https://aiintegraterz.com/certificationjourney in some form. Free-help framing must be honest, not "free-trial-of-paid-thing."

`positioning.md` — what the Capstone is, what makes it credible, identity to land
`offer.md` — what the host gets, what they commit to, the honest after-relationship line
`voice.md` — universal AI Integraterz voice rules (Justin as author)
`audience.md` — pain points + triggers + disqualifying signals
`write-rules.md` — hard constraints on Capstone emails (replaces sequences.md — no templates here)
