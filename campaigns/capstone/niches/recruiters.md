# Capstone — Recruiters niche overlay

## Why Capstone for recruiters

Capstone is the high-trust, slower-burn offer: a single, premium engagement
where we sit beside the agency owner and rebuild one operational area
end-to-end (usually outbound BD or sourcing). It's the right fit when the
recruiter is sophisticated, has revenue, and doesn't want a productized AICO.

## Framing

- Not a SaaS, not a course — a hands-on 30-day rebuild of one workflow
- We pick ONE bottleneck (sourcing OR BD OR client-reporting), not all three
- Deliverable is a working system + the recruiter's team trained on it

## Hook angles

- **Variant A** — "Pick one bottleneck. I'll spend 30 days rebuilding it with
  your team. Worth a call?"
- **Variant B** — "You don't need a tool. You need someone to sit next to your
  ops lead and ship the change. Curious?"

## Red lines

- Don't pitch this to <$1M agencies — they want the productized AICO instead
- No menu of services — Capstone is one engagement, one outcome

## Soft CTA

"15 min to see if there's a fit?" — no Loom, no asset. This one is
relationship-first.
