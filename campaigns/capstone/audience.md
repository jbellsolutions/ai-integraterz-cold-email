# Capstone Audience

## Ideal host business

- SMB, 1–30 employees, generating revenue
- Has at least one well-defined operational problem an AICO could meaningfully address in a 4-to-8-week project
- Owner or operator is willing to participate (1-hour intake, weekly 30-min check-in, end-of-project honest feedback)
- Comfortable with the fact that this is a learning context (Justin in the background, but candidate doing the work)
- Open to a longer project window than the paid 30-day sprint — typically 6–10 weeks

## Pain points and triggers worth referencing

The Research squad should mine for at least ONE of these per prospect. Email cannot send without one.

| Trigger | Where to find it | Why it works for Capstone |
|---|---|---|
| Hiring for a role AI could substantially handle (admin, intake, scheduling, content, support) | Active job posting | Exact substitution match — Capstone solves the hire |
| Public post about a workflow problem | LinkedIn / Twitter / blog | Owner is signaling specifically; we can quote the post |
| Recent expansion (multi-location, new hire, new product, new service line) | Press / LinkedIn / careers page | Ops drag is real and recent |
| Mention of trying AI tools that "almost work" | LinkedIn comments, podcast appearances | Tool-fatigue prospect — they want a person, not another tool |
| Active job posting that names a workflow we have a role kit for | LinkedIn Jobs / careers page | Highest signal — we can scope the Capstone before the call |

## Disqualifying signals (do not email)

- Pre-revenue / zero employees / no real workflow to integrate
- Already deeply embedded with another agency on the same problem
- Problem too vague to scope in a 1-hour intake
- Industries our candidates have not been trained for (default to AI Integraterz niche map)
- Anyone on the AI Integraterz "do not pitch" list (gambling, adult, restricted cannabis, weapons)
- Anyone who has previously unsubscribed from any AI Integraterz list
- Junior individual contributors with no buying authority
- Solo individuals with no business entity

## "They are visibly looking for a sales call, not free help" exception

The doc explicitly says: *"the agent should still send if other signals are strong; we'll catch the mismatch on the intake."* So this is a soft disqualifier — the Approver does NOT block on this alone if a strong public_signal or trigger_event is present.
