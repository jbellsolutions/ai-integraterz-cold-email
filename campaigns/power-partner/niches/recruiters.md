# Power Partner — Recruiters niche overlay

## Why this niche

Recruiting agency owners sit on top of a high-trust client list of operators
who are actively buying ways to grow revenue without adding headcount. They
are the perfect partner channel for AICO: they introduce, we install, the
recruiter earns recurring rev-share without changing their core business.

## The "recruiter stack" framing

The pitch is a stack, not a product:
1. **Recruiter Stack** — AI-powered candidate sourcing + outreach systems we
   install for the recruiter themselves first. Free setup. They get a faster
   pipeline before they ever hear "and there's a partner side too."
2. **Sales page + offer** — once their own ops are running, we co-build a
   white-label offer page they pitch to their existing client list.
3. **AICO for their clients** — partner intros become installs. They earn
   rev-share on every client AICO they bring in.

Lead with what's in it for THEM, not the partner side. Partner side lands in
email 2 once we've earned the right.

## Hook angles (variant menu)

- **Variant A** — "I built an AI sourcing stack for [agency-style] recruiters.
  It's free to install. If it works for you, there's a paid partner side."
- **Variant B** — "Your clients already pay you to find people. What if you
  could also charge them to install AI for the people you find?"
- **Variant C** — Give-first. "Here's a 12-min Loom of the AICO build I'd
  install in a recruiting firm. No pitch. If it's useful, reply."

## Signal criteria for Research squad

Strong signal:
- Recent hire-volume posts ("placed 40 in Q1")
- Niche specialization (legal, healthcare, exec search) — easier to map AICO
- LinkedIn posts about "hiring is broken" / AI in hiring
- Podcast appearances on agency growth

Weak signal: generic "we connect great people" copy with no specifics.

## Red lines

- Never lead with the partner/rev-share side. That's email 2.
- No "transform your recruiting firm" — they hate consultant talk.
- Don't pitch AI replacing recruiters. Pitch AI making placements stickier.

## Soft CTA

Email 1: "Worth a 12-min Loom of what I'd install in [their firm]?"
Email 2: Loom + reveal partner side.
Email 3: Booking link if no reply.
