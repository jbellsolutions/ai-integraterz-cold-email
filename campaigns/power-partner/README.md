# Power Partner Campaign

Jay-Abraham-style partner recruitment. Agencies that already serve our ICP get a free AI integrator deployed in their business; in exchange they introduce clients (no selling, just intros) and earn $1,000 per placement + $150/month recurring per client.

**The frame shift:** vendor → collaborator. Don't pitch the prospect's clients — pitch the prospect themselves on a peer-to-peer revenue split where we deploy before they have to commit anything.

## When the Strategy squad picks this angle

Auto-pick when:
- ICP = agency owners, consultants, fractional CMOs, or anyone with a roster of B2B clients in our addressable verticals
- Prospect has 15+ active clients (qualifying filter — Marshall)
- Prospect has not historically been a buyer of AI services themselves

## How the Copy squad uses this folder

`positioning.md` — what UAIS is and the identity shift to land
`offer.md` — exact terms (free integrator + $1K + $150/mo + guarantees + protection clause)
`voice.md` — words to use, words to avoid, brand voice attributes
`swipe-file.md` — 10 headline subject lines + 10 hook arsenal lines + A/B testing protocol
`sequences.md` — 3 production-approved sequences (A: Partnership, B: Revenue, C: Free Integrator), 9 emails total
`source/round-3-final-approved.md` — full Titans-Council-approved deliverable (canonical)

The Copy squad is instructed to **adapt, not copy verbatim** — these are templates with personalization variables. The per-prospect signal from the Research squad replaces the generic positioning hook in Email 1.
