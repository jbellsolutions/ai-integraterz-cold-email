# Power Partner Swipe File

10 headlines + 10 hook lines + A/B test protocol. Production-approved by Titans Council. Use as templates — Copy squad personalizes per prospect using the Research squad's signal.

## Headline Battery (subject lines, all <50 chars)

| # | Subject | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `{firstName}, quick question about your agency` | First-touch, neutral, high open rate |
| 2 | `Partnership idea for {companyName}` | Sequence A Email 1 — peer-to-peer frame |
| 3 | `{firstName}, quick math on your client base` | Sequence B Email 1 — math-forward |
| 4 | `Not a pitch. A revenue split.` | Anti-pitch positioning in 5 words |
| 5 | `{firstName}, free person in your business (not a cult thing)` | Sequence C Email 1 — humor pattern interrupt |
| 6 | `$1K per intro + $150/mo recurring` | Specificity + recurring in subject |
| 7 | `Your clients are asking about AI` | Pain-point opener |
| 8 | `We deploy before we ask` | Mechanism-led differentiator |
| 9 | `{firstName}, a recurring revenue question` | Appeals to MRR-thinking owners |
| 10 | `{firstName}, agencies in your market next week` | Competitive urgency, not threat |

## Hook Arsenal (first 1-2 sentences — where replies are won)

| # | Hook | Use For |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | "I deploy AI integrators into businesses. You have clients who need them. I'd rather pay you $1,000 per intro than cold email them myself." | Seq B Email 1 |
| 2 | "Most partnership programs ask you to sell something you've never used. We do the opposite — we put the whole system in your business first, free, before we mention the word 'referral.'" | Seq A Email 1 |
| 3 | "I'm going to skip the part where I pretend I found your website fascinating. I found your agency because you have clients, and I want to pay you to introduce them to us." | Seq C Email 1 |
| 4 | "Your clients are going to hire an AI integrator in the next 12 months. The question is whether they get it from you or from someone who cold emails them." | Email 2 or 3 |
| 5 | "Quick math: you have clients. Each introduction pays you $1,000 + $150/month recurring. We handle everything else." | Seq B Email 1 |
| 6 | "Two questions: Are your clients asking about AI? Do you have a good answer? If it's 'yes' and 'not really' — that's why I'm writing." | Highest-reply hook for problem-aware |
| 7 | "This isn't a referral program. We put an AI integrator in your business first — free — and you earn every month for every client you introduce." | Seq A Email 1 |
| 8 | "I'll be direct — I want to deploy a free AI integrator into your agency this month. No strings. You experience the results. Then we talk about whether your clients need the same thing." | Seq C Email 1 |
| 9 | "I'm building a partner network with 20 agencies — not affiliates, not referral sources, but revenue partners who earn every month. We have a few spots open in your market." | Seq A Email 1 |
| 10 | "I don't want to sell you anything. I want to pay you $1,000 every time you say six words to a client: 'Let me introduce you to someone.'" | Most memorable reduction |

## A/B Test Protocol

| Test | A | B | Min Sample |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | #1 Curiosity | #2 Partnership | 200/variant |
| 2 | #3 Math | #9 Recurring | 200/variant |
| 3 | #5 Humor | #7 Pain-point | 200/variant |
| 4 | #4 Anti-pitch | #6 Specificity | 200/variant |
| 5 | #8 Mechanism | #10 Competitive | 200/variant |

Run each minimum 2 weeks. Measure open at 24h AND **reply at 72h** — opens without replies optimize the wrong metric.
