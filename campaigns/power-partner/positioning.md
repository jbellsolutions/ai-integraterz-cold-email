# Power Partner Positioning

## What UAIS does (1-line)

We deploy AI integrators — a trained human paired with AI agents — into businesses. 7-day deployment. Doubles output in 30 days, guaranteed.

## Identity shift to land in the agency owner's mind

From: *"a vendor who wants to sell my clients something"*
To: *"a peer who pays me to make introductions while we both make money"*

## Category UAIS owns

**AI Fulfillment Infrastructure for Agencies.**

Not an AI tool. Not a referral program. Not an agency partnership. We are the deployment layer that turns "your clients should use AI" into "your clients are using AI, here is the recurring check."

## Primary message

You have clients who need AI help. You are not equipped to deliver it. We are. Don't compete with us for their attention — partner with us, deploy our system in your own business first to see it work, then introduce clients and earn recurring revenue while we handle every minute of fulfillment.

## Why this works

Agency owners get pitched AI tools 15× per quarter. They tune out. The pattern interrupt is: **we put the system in your business first, free, with no ask.** Once they experience it, the introduction conversation is structurally easy — they are recommending what they already use.

## Awareness levels (deploy-by-awareness)

- **Unaware** (no AI strategy): lead with "your clients are about to ask"
- **Problem-aware** (knows clients want AI, no answer): lead with "we are the answer you can offer them"
- **Solution-aware** (knows AI exists): lead with the recurring revenue mechanism
- **Most-aware** (already evaluating partnerships): lead with the free deployment differentiator

## What the Copy squad must preserve

1. Always pay the partner first (free deploy) before asking for an intro
2. Specific dollar numbers in the first email ($1K + $150/mo recurring)
3. Qualifying filter visible (15+ active clients) — selectiveness is the frame
4. No language that sounds like an affiliate program (see voice.md)
