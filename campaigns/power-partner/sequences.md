# Power Partner Sequences (3 sequences × 3 emails = 9 templates)

These are templates. Copy squad must:
1. Replace `{first_name}` / `{company}` with prospect data
2. Inject the Research squad's per-prospect signal as a contextual line in Email 1 (replacing generic opening if signal is strong enough — Tier S/A signals only)
3. Run the AI-slop critic against every output
4. Rotate spintax variants `{a|b|c}` to avoid identical-content flags at >100/day

## Email Standards (HARD RULES)

- Email 1: <125 words, no links, no bold, no HTML, reply-based CTA only
- Email 2: <150 words, ONE link max (calendar OR one-pager, not both), no bold
- Email 3: <125 words, calendar link allowed, no bold
- All emails: plain text, no images, no logo signature, first-name sign-off (`-- Justin`)
- P.S.: one sentence max — top objection or curiosity burst

## Emotional Arc

- Email 1 = **Curiosity** ("here's what's possible")
- Email 2 = **Conviction** ("here's why it's real" — must contain NEW info, not restatement)
- Email 3 = **Consequence** ("here's what happens if you act / don't")

## SEQUENCE A — The Partnership Recruitment

Lead with the partnership frame and the mechanism.

### A1 — Day 1

Subject A: `Partnership idea for {company}`
Subject B: `{first_name}, quick question about your agency`

Body:
```
{first_name},

I {run|founded|lead} UAIS — we deploy AI integrators into businesses. A trained human paired with AI agents. Doubles output in 30 days, guaranteed.

I'm not writing to sell you this.

I'm writing because {your agency has|{company} has} clients who need AI help — and I'd rather partner with you to serve them than compete for their attention.

The model: we put an AI integrator in your business first, free. You experience it. Then you introduce clients who need the same thing. You earn $1,000 per placement + $150/mo recurring. We handle all fulfillment.

We're building this with 20 agencies that have 15+ active clients. If that's {company}, worth a 10-min call?

-- Justin

P.S. We deploy in your business before we ask for anything. That's what makes this different.
```

### A2 — Day 3 (`Re: partnership idea for {company}`)

```
{first_name},

One thing I didn't mention — the recurring piece.

Most partner programs pay you once. We pay you every month. $150/mo per active client, for as long as they stay. Average client stays 14+ months. Six introductions over six months = $900/month by month six. The income stacks.

On top of that: we guarantee doubled output in 30 days or money back. 7-day deployment. Zero lock-in.

Your reputation is protected. Your clients get results or they pay nothing.

The agencies figuring out the AI answer for their clients right now are going to keep those relationships. The rest will watch someone else become the trusted advisor.

Still worth a conversation?

-- Justin
```

### A3 — Day 7 (`Closing the loop — {first_name}`)

```
{first_name},

Last note from me.

I've reached out twice about a revenue partnership between {company} and UAIS. If the timing is wrong, I respect that.

Here's what's on the table: free AI integrator in your business, $1,000 per client placement, $150/mo recurring per client, zero fulfillment on your end.

We're filling partner spots in your market this month. Once filled, I'll be reaching out to other agencies in your vertical.

Not a threat — just how it works. The agencies that move first get the territory.

If there's a 20% chance this makes sense, 10 minutes is a fair trade.

-- Justin

P.S. Either way, I hope your agency finds the AI answer for your clients. That question isn't going away.
```

Lead with the money and the economics.

```

{I'll get straight to it.|Straight to the point.}

I {run|lead} UAIS. We deploy AI integrators — a trained human + AI agents. 7 days to deploy. Doubles output. $2K per placement.

The deal: you make an intro. We handle everything — the call, the deployment, the training, the support. I'll also put a free AI integrator in your business first.

We're recruiting {10|15} agencies with 15+ active clients this quarter.

Worth a 10-min call?

-- Justin

P.S. Five introductions = $5,000 + $750/month recurring. That's the math on sending five emails.
```

```
Following up with one number that reframes this.

$150 per month, per introduction. That's what you earn — every month — for connecting a client to us. It doesn't reset. It stacks.

Six introductions over six months: $900/month by month six. $10,800 over the year. From six conversations with people who already trust you.

We guarantee your clients double their output in 30 days or money back. Zero risk to your reputation.

10 minutes to see if {company}'s math works?
```

## SEQUENCE C — The Free Integrator (humor + give-first)

Lead with the gift and the demonstration.

### C1 — Day 1

Subject A: `{first_name}, free person in your business (not a cult thing)`
Subject B: `We deploy before we ask`

```
{first_name},

{I'll be upfront:|Honest disclosure:} this is a cold email. I found {your agency|{company}} because you have clients who probably need AI help.

Here's what makes this different from the other partnership emails in your inbox:

I want to put a trained AI integrator into your business. Free. A real human paired with AI agents — deployed in 7 days, not 3 months.

Why free? Because I know what happens next. Your output doubles. Your clients notice. And when one asks "how are you doing this?" you say "let me introduce you to the team that set it up."

That introduction pays you $1,000 + $150/mo recurring. We handle everything else.

If that sounds interesting, reply "tell me more." If it sounds insane, reply "you're insane." I'll respond to either.

-- Justin

P.S. We're building this with agencies that have 15+ clients. Not everyone qualifies.
```

### C2 — Day 3 (`Re: the free AI integrator`)

```
{first_name},

Quick follow-up — not to restate, but to add something I left out.

Every partner earns monthly. Not once. $150/mo per client you introduce, for as long as they stay. The average client stays 14+ months. Six introductions and you're earning $900/month from conversations you were already having.

The guarantees on your reputation: 30-day double-output or money back. 7-day deployment. Zero lock-in.

And you still have the free integrator running in your own business — making your agency faster while your competitors are still Googling "how to implement AI."

Reply "I'm in" and I'll set up a quick call to deploy yours this week.

-- Justin
```

### C3 — Day 7 (`Last one from me, {first_name}`)

```
{first_name},

Third email. I respect your inbox too much to send a fourth.

Recap: free AI integrator in your business. $1,000 per client you introduce. $150/month recurring. We handle everything.

If you didn't reply:

A) Bad timing. Fair. This'll be here.
B) Suspicious. Also fair. I can prove it on a 10-min call.
C) Not interested. No hard feelings whatsoever.

In 6 months, every agency in your market will be scrambling to answer the AI question. I'd rather it be you that has the answer — and the revenue stream that comes with it.

-- Justin

P.S. The ones who said yes all told me the same thing: "I almost didn't reply."
```

## Sequence Selection (Strategy squad picks one of A/B/C per ICP segment)

| Segment | Best Sequence | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Marketing/digital agencies (15+ clients) | **A** Partnership | Identity + peer framing lands; they think in terms of partner stacks |
| Lead-gen / paid-ads agencies | **B** Revenue | Money-forward; these owners think in CAC/LTV math |
| Boutique/solo consultants, fractional CMOs | **C** Free Integrator | Lower volume, higher trust play; humor + give-first wins |

Default if uncertain: **A** (Partnership) — broadest fit and the council's primary recommendation.
