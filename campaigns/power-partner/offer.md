# Power Partner Offer

## The deal (1-line)

Free AI integrator deployed in the partner's own business. Partner introduces clients. Partner earns $1,000 per placement + $150/month recurring per client, for as long as the client stays.

## Stack

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| Free AI integrator | Deployed into partner's business in 7 days. Trained human + AI agents. Real value before any ask. |
| Per-placement bounty | $1,000 paid to partner on each client placement |
| Recurring revenue share | $150/month per active client, indefinite (continues as long as client stays) |
| 30-day output guarantee | 2× client output in 30 days or money back. Protects partner's reputation. |
| 7-day deployment SLA | Fast enough that the client experiences value inside one billing cycle |
| Zero fulfillment burden | Partner does intro only. We run the call, deployment, training, support, billing. |

## Partner economics (real math)

| Intros | Per-placement | Recurring (mo 1) | Recurring (mo 6) | Yr 1 cumulative |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 6 over 6 months | $6,000 | $150 | $900 | ~$16,800 |
| 12 over 12 months | $12,000 | $150 | $1,800 (mo 12) | ~$23,400 |

Average client stays 14+ months → recurring stack compounds.

## UAIS economics (why it works for both sides)

- LTV per client ≈ $24K+ (24mo × $1K avg)
- CAC via partner = $1,000 + 6 × $150 (~$1,900 in first 6mo) vs $400-800 cold-email CAC
- BUT partner-channel close rate is 5-10× higher and sales cycle is ~3× shorter
- Net contribution per partner client > direct-channel client even after the higher CAC

## Risk reversal

- Money-back guarantee on 30-day output doubling
- Zero lock-in — client can leave anytime
- Free deploy in partner's business is the trust-purchase

## Free Integrator Sustainability Clause (90-day review)

Partner must introduce ≥1 qualified client within 90 days of free deployment. Otherwise UAIS reserves the right to convert the deployment to paid at standard rate or pause it. Protects UAIS from non-referring partners becoming a cost center. **Mention only if asked or in the partner agreement — not in cold email.**

## Partner Protection Clause

UAIS will not solicit partner's clients for any service outside the AI Integrator engagement. Commission continuity survives partner termination. Non-poaching of partner's account managers. **Reference if asked the "what stops you from going direct?" objection — see voice.md objection handling.**

## Qualification criteria

Partner qualifies if:
- 15+ active B2B clients
- Owns the client relationship (not a sub-contractor)
- In a vertical adjacent to ours, not directly competing
- Owner/founder is the prospect (not a junior BD person)
