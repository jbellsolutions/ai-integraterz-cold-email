## Pain points and triggers worth referencing

- Content cadence breaking — the expert posted two months ago and nothing since
- Sales conversations slipping because they cannot keep up with inbound
- Ad budget burning while they are on the road or in client work
- Cannot hire a full-time team yet, but a single VA has not stuck
- They have tried VAs and given up — VA churn at month 2 or 3
