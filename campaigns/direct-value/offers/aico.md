> "Reduce costs by up to 50%, increase productivity by up to 50%, in 30 days. Run forever by an AICO inside your business."

## Who it is for

- SMB owners, **1-30 employees**, generating revenue
- Owners who understand AI is inevitable and want to lead the transition rather than watch it happen
- Businesses with real workflows worth building on — sales, ops, admin, marketing
- Owner is the decision maker and can show up for the sprint

## The six deliverables (what the sprint produces)

1. **Custom AI Roadmap** — every role, every department, every level mapped in one intake call. Built for the business, not a template.
2. **Role Kits — every employee** — custom SOP, prompt toolkit, daily checklist, 30-day adoption challenge for each person's specific job.
3. **Full AI Ecosystem Training** — Claude + Codex + Gemini + Perplexity + SuperSet + n8n + Make + Zapier. Complete ecosystem, not just one platform.
4. **AICO placed in the business** — hired, trained, and managed by us. Drives adoption from the inside. Stays after the sprint ends.
5. **Department AI Game Plans** — Sales, Ops, Marketing, Admin each get their own AI playbook with specific workflows, tools, and prompts.
6. **30-Day Implementation Sprint** — weekly coaching through the full build. We stay until adoption is locked in, then hand off to the AICO.

## Pain points and triggers worth referencing

- Burning cycles on workflows AI could substantially handle (intake, scheduling, content, support, prior-auth, recall, dispatch)
- Owner is the bottleneck on operational decisions and has no AI-native person on the team
- Tried VAs; the VAs did not stick. Pattern: VA churn at month 2 or 3
- Tried AI tools that "almost work" but no one on staff has time to deploy and evolve them
- Recently expanded — multi-location, new hire, new product — and ops capacity did not scale with it
- Hiring for a role AI could meaningfully augment or substitute for

## Disqualifying signals

- Pre-revenue startups with no team or workflows to integrate
- Owners looking for a course or a tool, not a hire
- Owners expecting results without any team participation
- Anyone unwilling to commit to a dedicated AICO role inside the business
