# Direct Value (AICO / Expert Series) — Recruiters niche overlay

## Why this niche for AICO direct

Recruiting agencies have repeatable workflows (sourcing, screening, outreach,
client reporting) that AICO replaces or accelerates. They feel pipeline pain
acutely and can quantify ROI per placement, so the math conversation lands fast.

## Framing for AICO direct (not the partner play)

This overlay is for the *direct* AICO pitch — we're selling the build to the
recruiting firm itself, not to their clients. Power-Partner has a separate
overlay for the partner motion.

Lead with the operational pain:
- Sourcers spending hours on LinkedIn that an agent can do overnight
- Outreach reply rates capped because every BD email looks the same
- Client reporting that eats Friday afternoons

## Expert Series angle

For agency *owners* who already teach their playbook (podcast, course,
LinkedIn content) — the Expert Series productizes their IP into an AI agent
they sell or give to clients. Higher LTV than a single AICO build.

Strong signal: course, paid newsletter, podcast, conference speaking history.

## Hook angles

- **Variant A (AICO)** — "Built an AI sourcing agent for [niche] recruiters.
  It runs overnight, hands the recruiter a shortlist by 8am. 14-day build."
- **Variant B (AICO)** — Reverse the usual "we save you time" — lead with
  *throughput*: "You'll send 3x the qualified outbounds without hiring."
- **Variant C (Expert Series)** — "You've got a placement methodology. Let's
  turn it into an agent your clients license."

## Red lines

- Don't pitch as "AI recruiter replacement" — they sell trust, not bodies.
- Avoid "transform" / "revolutionize" — recruiter inboxes are saturated.
- No fake personalization ("noticed you placed at X") unless it's real.

## Soft CTA

Loom-first. "Here's a 12-min walkthrough of the AICO I'd build for [firm].
Reply 'send' if useful."
