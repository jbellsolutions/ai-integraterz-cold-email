# AI Integraterz Positioning (Universal)

## Brand 1-line

AI Integraterz places **AICOs (AI Chief Officers)** inside small and mid-market businesses, then runs a **30-day implementation sprint** to build the operating system the AICO will run forever. Every placement is overseen by **Justin Bellware — Microsoft-certified AI CO, AI CIO, AI CTO.** We do not take a cut of the AICO's hourly rate; clients pay the AICO direct.

## What makes us different

1. **Microsoft-certified oversight on every placement.** The AICO is backed by someone who has actually architected AI transformations at scale.
2. **Structured 30-day implementation sprint built into the placement.** End of sprint = AICO running a fully built operating system, not figuring out where to start.
3. **AI-native vetted talent** — not generic VAs who took a ChatGPT course. Pre-trained on Claude, Claude Code, n8n, Make, Zapier, Gemini, Codex, Perplexity, SuperSet.
4. **Proprietary AICO audit methodology + pre-built role kits** — custom SOPs, prompt toolkits, daily checklists, 30-day adoption challenges — that the AICO deploys from day one.

## How clients pay (critical for cold-reply handling)

We never take a cut of the AICO's hourly rate. Three separate flows of money:

| What | Who they pay | Estimate |
|---|---|---|
| Placement + Sprint | AI Integraterz | One-time fee, varies by offer |
| AICO hourly after placement | The AICO directly (client payrolls them) | $10–$20 / hour |
| Mastermind & ongoing support | AI Integraterz | $500 / month, cancel anytime |

If a prospect asks for price in a cold reply, the answer is: **"We will scope it on the audit call."** Don't quote in cold.

## How Strategy squad picks the sub-offer

Map the lead's segment to the sub-offer. If multiple fit, the larger/more sales-driven side wins.

| Lead profile | Sub-offer |
|---|---|
| SMB 1–30 employees, generating revenue, owner wants AI across the whole business | **AICO** |
| Solopreneur expert / coach / course creator / newsletter / podcaster / community owner | **Expert Series** |
| Sales-driven SMB (recruiting, real estate, mortgage, insurance, marketing agency, sales coaching, auto dealer) | **Sovereign Blueprint** |

Sizing constraint:
- **Sovereign Blueprint requires ≥10 employees** — disqualify smaller for this offer (either downshift to Expert Series or upshift to AICO depending on shape)
- **Expert Series requires ≤50 employees** — disqualify larger (upshift to AICO)

## Identity shift to land in the prospect's mind

From: *"another vendor pitching AI tools"*
To: *"a Microsoft-certified operator who places someone inside my business who actually runs the system"*

The credibility anchor is the placement + the sprint, not the technology stack.
